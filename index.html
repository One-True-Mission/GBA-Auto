About GBA

Father & Son
Built for the Road

Garage Built Automotive LLC is a father and son mobile mechanic team based in Kyle, TX. We built GBA around a simple idea: car trouble shouldn't mean towing hassles, long waits, or overpriced shop bills. We load up the truck and come to you — at home, at work, or wherever your vehicle is sitting.

We work on all makes and models and handle everything from quick brake pad swaps to full engine services. Whether you're in Kyle, across the greater Austin area, or up in Waco and the surrounding communities, our mobile team is ready to roll.

Oil Changes & Maintenance

Routine oil changes, filter replacements, and fluid services done right at your driveway or parking spot. Fast, clean, and no shop trip required.

Brake Service

Engine Repair

From minor tune-ups to more extensive engine work, we arrive equipped to diagnose and repair. All makes and models, foreign and domestic.

Diagnostics

Check engine lights, fault codes, and strange behavior — we bring diagnostic tools to your location and get to the bottom of the problem fast.

Battery & Electrical

Dead battery, bad alternator, starter issues — we test and replace on-site so you're not stuck waiting on a tow or a jump that doesn't last.

Cooling System
